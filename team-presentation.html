The Mission

AI assists.
Humans decide.

Creative teams lose weeks translating client calls, transcripts, and briefs into structured project intelligence. Synthos collapses that gap — turning scattered conversations into actionable strategy, proposals, and quotes.

AI Layer

Reads transcripts, extracts wants, objectives, constraints, risks, and action items automatically.

Human Layer

Reviews drafts, refines direction, and approves every deliverable before it reaches a client.

Design System

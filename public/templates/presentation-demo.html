Summary
The client discussed their vision for a portfolio website that reflects their transition from music/video production into AI and software engineering. They want a design that feels contemporary but timeless, with smooth interactions and clear content hierarchy. The team aligned on a minimalist dark-theme approach with subtle motion design.
